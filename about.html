Hi, I'm Pallavi.

I'm a UX Designer and a graduate student in Human Computer Interaction at the University of Michigan.

Obsessively doodling on the back covers of notebooks since childhood, design has always been a saviour for me and continues to be one today. I'm particularly fascinated with personalisation and customisation in UX, and the process that goes into making experiences not just seamless, but delightful. In my spare time, I enjoy swimming, sketching and chasing after dogs trying to pet them. You might also find me in a stationery store buying notebooks that are too beautiful to actually use.

I love to work on fresh ideas and clean, well designed pages are my source of instant gratification. If you want to talk about my work or anything else, please send me an email, give me a call or reach out to me at any of my social media handles!
